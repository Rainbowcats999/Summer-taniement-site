Normally, this would be the first ride that people go on, but for me it ended up being my last. It has big drops and gives you butterflies.

Green Lantern:

This was the very first rollercoaster I went on at sixflags, it had loops and went fast, gave me butterflies. Also the fact that you stand up while riding is a little concerning.

Ride now!
King Da Ka:

This was my personal favorite, did you know it's the tallest and fastest rollercoaster in the world, crazy right? It starts off slow and then just shoots you 500 feet into the air. But what's even crazier is that you don't feel any butterflies in your stomach because it's so fast, the ride lasts approximately 30 seconds and is over in the blink of an eye.

Ride now!
El Toro:

This one was scary. Massive drops, and you can literally hear the wood breaking as you're riding. And as soon as it's done you feel like you need an entire neck adjustment.

Ride now!
